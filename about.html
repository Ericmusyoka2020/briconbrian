Our Story

Founded in 2010, bricon Building Company Limited has been at the forefront of construction excellence in East Africa. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted construction companies.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of hundreds of clients across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. We believe in building not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients.
